About Me

Experienced Unity Developer with Strong Knowledge of C#, OOP, and Clean Code Principles. I have expertise in developing games, XR applications, and a wide variety of tools used by game designers and other team members.

My knowledge of SOLID and KISS principles ensures that my code is clean, efficient, and maintainable. I have extensive experience in working with popular Unity packages like Odin inspector, DoTween, and different AR libraries.

In addition to my Unity skills, I am also experienced in Cocos Creator and TypeScript, and have worked with Photton, URP, and lighting techniques. I am familiar with UnitTest using NUnit and Unity Test Runner.

My portfolio includes projects for Windows, Web, Android, iOS, UWP, Hololens, and Oculus DK2. I am an effective communicator and team player, with strong problem-solving skills and a critical mindset.
